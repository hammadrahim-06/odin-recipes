Beef Biryani

Description

Aromatic and flavorful beef biryani made with tender meat, basmati rice, and spices.

This dish is a celebration of South Asian cuisine, ideal for festive meals or weekends.

Ingredients

  • Beef, cut into cubes
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions, tomatoes, yogurt
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Biryani masala, turmeric, chili powder
  • Mint leaves, coriander, green chilies
  • Oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Marinate beef with spices and yogurt. Let sit for 1 hour.
  2. Cook marinated beef until tender.
  3. Fry onions separately until golden and mix with cooked beef.
  4. Boil rice until 70% cooked, then layer with beef mixture.
  5. Steam (“dum”) for 20-30 minutes. Serve hot.
