Beef Steak

Description

This juicy beef steak is pan-seared to perfection with a flavorful garlic butter baste. It’s tender, rich, and full of bold, meaty flavor — perfect for a quick dinner or special occasion.

Paired best with mashed potatoes or sautéed veggies, this steak recipe is simple yet delivers restaurant-quality taste from your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 beef steak (sirloin or ribeye)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Steps

  1. Bring steak to room temperature and season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear steak 2–3 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  3. In the last minute of cooking, add butter, garlic, and herbs to the pan and baste the steak.
  4. Remove steak and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
