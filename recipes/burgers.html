Chicken Burger

Home

Description

This zinger-style chicken burger features a crispy, golden-fried chicken patty packed with bold flavor and crunch. It's layered with crisp iceberg lettuce and spicy jalapeños, all tucked inside a soft bun.

A perfect balance of heat and texture, this burger skips the traditional tomatoes and onions for a spicier, more focused flavor. It's a quick, satisfying meal that's easy to make at home and tastes just like your favorite fast-food chicken burger.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast, flattened
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • Breadcrumbs (for coating)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes or paprika
  • Mayonnaise
  • Jalapeño slices
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Burger buns
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Flatten the chicken breast and season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  2. Coat the chicken in flour, dip in beaten egg, and cover in breadcrumbs.
  3. Deep-fry or shallow-fry the chicken until golden and crispy. Drain excess oil.
  4. Lightly toast the burger buns.
  5. Spread mayonnaise on the buns, place the crispy chicken, add jalapeños and iceberg lettuce.
  6. Close the burger and serve hot.
